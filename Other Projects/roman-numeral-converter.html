Roman Numeral Converter



A Roman numeral converter is a tool or program designed to convert numbers between the Arabic numeral system (commonly used in everyday mathematics) and the Roman numeral system. Roman numerals use a combination of letters from the Latin alphabet, such as I, V, X, L, C, D, and M, to represent numeric values. The converter simplifies the process of translating numbers from one system to the other, making it convenient for users to work with Roman numerals or Arabic numerals depending on their needs. This tool is particularly useful for tasks like deciphering historical dates, numbering chapters or sections, and understanding Roman numeral representations in various contexts.
