Guess The Number

Details

Explanation

  • When You will Start The Game, Our Backend Will generate a random number between 1 and 100, and You Have To guess it.
  • When You will make the first try our Backend Will Tell You whether The Number is greator or smaller than a generated number.
  • You Will Keep Guessing and at last You will Enter The Correct Number.

Example

  • Suppose Our Engine has generated 47.
  • When The Game Starts You Will Enter Your First Guess. Suppos you entered 50.
  • Now Our Engine Will Tell That You have entered a greater number.
  • Now suppose you will enter 40.
  • Our Engine will tell you that you have entered a smaller number now.
  • Now You will enter 45, suppose.
  • Our Engine Will again tell you that you have entered smaller number.
  • Now You will enter a bigger number than 45 and may be at this try you will enter 47.
  • After You will guess the right number your score will be printed.
  • Your Score will depend on the number of tries you have taken to guess the number.
